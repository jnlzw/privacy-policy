English Policy

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the PhoPick iOS app and related app extensions. PhoPick helps you review, compare, and clean up photos in your Apple Photos library.

2. Information We Access

With your permission, PhoPick accesses your photo library so it can display photos, group similar photos, show photo details, mark items for review, and delete photos only after you confirm that action.

The app may process photo metadata such as creation dates, modification dates, local asset identifiers, thumbnails, and derived similarity information. This processing is used to provide the app's cleanup features.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect your photos, videos, photo library contents, personal account information, advertising identifiers, or location data on our servers.

PhoPick may store app preferences, cleanup progress, achievement progress, purchase entitlement status, and similar app state locally on your device. Some local app data may be shared between the app and its widget through Apple's App Group mechanism.

4. Photo Processing and Deletion

Photo analysis is performed on device. PhoPick does not upload your photos for cloud analysis. When you choose to delete photos, the deletion is performed through Apple's Photos framework and requires your confirmation. Deleted photos may still be recoverable from the Photos app's Recently Deleted album according to Apple's Photos behavior.

5. Purchases and Subscriptions

PhoPick may offer subscriptions or in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. Apple processes payments, renewals, refunds, and payment credentials. PhoPick uses StoreKit to check whether you have an active entitlement, but we do not receive or store your payment card details.

6. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

PhoPick does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not show ads, and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. The app does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.

7. Diagnostics and Support

The app may create local diagnostic logs to help understand app behavior, including purchase loading or photo cleanup status. These logs are stored locally unless you choose to share information with us when requesting support. If you contact us by email, we will use the information you provide only to respond to your request.

8. Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it through app features, reset relevant app state, or uninstall the app. Purchase records are managed by Apple according to Apple's policies.

9. Your Choices

  • You can grant, limit, or revoke photo library access in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove app data stored locally on your device, subject to iOS backup and system behavior.

10. Children

PhoPick is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes or when legal or platform requirements change. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

中文隐私政策

1. 适用范围

本隐私政策适用于 PhoPick iOS App 及相关 App 扩展。PhoPick 是一款照片便捷清理工具，用于帮助你浏览、对比、分组和整理 Apple 照片图库中的照片。

2. 我们访问的信息

在你授权后，PhoPick 会访问你的照片图库，用于展示照片、识别相似照片、显示照片信息、标记待处理照片，并且只会在你确认后执行删除操作。

App 可能会在设备本地处理照片创建时间、修改时间、本地资源标识、缩略图以及相似度分析结果等信息。这些信息仅用于提供照片整理功能。

3. 我们收集的信息

我们不会在自己的服务器上收集你的照片、视频、照片图库内容、个人账号信息、广告标识符或位置信息。

PhoPick 可能会在你的设备本地保存 App 设置、整理进度、成就进度、购买权益状态以及类似的 App 状态数据。部分本地数据可能会通过 Apple 的 App Group 机制在 App 和小组件之间共享。

4. 照片处理与删除

照片分析在设备本地完成。PhoPick 不会把你的照片上传到云端进行分析。当你选择删除照片时，删除操作会通过 Apple Photos 框架执行，并需要你的确认。根据 Apple Photos 的系统行为，删除后的照片可能仍可在「最近删除」中恢复。

5. 购买与订阅

PhoPick 可能通过 Apple App Store 提供订阅或 App 内购买。付款、续订、退款和支付凭证由 Apple 处理。PhoPick 使用 StoreKit 检查你是否拥有有效权益，但我们不会接收或保存你的银行卡信息。

6. 分析、广告与追踪

PhoPick 不使用第三方广告 SDK，不展示广告，也不会跨其他公司拥有的 App 或网站追踪你。App 不会请求 App Tracking Transparency 追踪权限。

7. 诊断与支持

App 可能会在设备本地生成诊断日志，用于帮助了解 App 行为，例如购买加载状态或照片整理状态。除非你在寻求支持时主动提供信息，否则这些日志不会发送给我们。如果你通过邮件联系我们，我们只会使用你提供的信息来回复你的请求。

8. 数据保留

本地 App 数据会保留在你的设备上，直到你通过 App 功能删除、重置相关 App 状态，或卸载 App。购买记录由 Apple 根据其政策管理。

9. 你的选择

  • 你可以在 iOS 设置中授权、限制或撤销照片图库访问权限。
  • 你可以在 Apple ID 的订阅设置中管理或取消订阅。
  • 你可以卸载 App 来移除设备上的本地 App 数据，但这仍会受 iOS 备份和系统行为影响。

10. 儿童隐私

PhoPick 并非面向儿童的 App，我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

11. 政策更新

当 App 功能变化，或法律、平台要求发生变化时，我们可能会更新本隐私政策。最新版本会发布在本页面，并更新日期。
