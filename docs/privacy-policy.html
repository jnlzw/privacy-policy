PhoPick Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

PhoPick is a photo cleanup app. Photo analysis happens on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers, do not show ads, and do not track you across apps or websites.

English

1. Information We Access

With your permission, PhoPick accesses your Apple Photos library so you can review photos, compare similar photos, mark photos for cleanup, and delete photos after confirmation.

PhoPick may process photo metadata on your device, such as creation dates, modification dates, local asset identifiers, thumbnails, and similarity results. This information is used only to provide photo cleanup features.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect photos, videos, photo library contents, personal account information, advertising identifiers, or location data on our servers.

The app may store settings, cleanup progress, achievement progress, purchase entitlement status, and similar app state locally on your device.

3. Photo Processing

Photo analysis is performed on device. PhoPick does not upload your photos for cloud analysis. When you delete photos, the action is performed through Apple's Photos framework and requires your confirmation. Deleted photos may still appear in the Photos app's Recently Deleted album according to Apple's system behavior.

4. Purchases and Subscriptions

PhoPick may offer subscriptions or in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. Apple processes payments, renewals, refunds, and payment credentials. PhoPick uses StoreKit to check whether you have an active entitlement. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

5. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

PhoPick does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not show ads, and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

6. Diagnostics and Support

The app may create local diagnostic logs to help understand app behavior. These logs stay on your device unless you choose to share information with us when requesting support. If you contact us by email, we use the information you provide only to respond to your request.

7. Your Choices

  • You can grant, limit, or revoke photo library access in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID subscription settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove app data stored locally on your device, subject to iOS backup and system behavior.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at lzwmax@qq.com.

中文

1. 我们访问的信息

在你授权后，PhoPick 会访问你的 Apple 照片图库，用于浏览照片、对比相似照片、标记待清理照片，并在你确认后删除照片。

PhoPick 可能会在你的设备本地处理照片创建时间、修改时间、本地资源标识、缩略图和相似度分析结果等信息。这些信息仅用于提供照片清理功能。

2. 我们收集的信息

我们不会在自己的服务器上收集你的照片、视频、照片图库内容、个人账号信息、广告标识符或位置信息。

App 可能会在你的设备本地保存设置、整理进度、成就进度、购买权益状态以及类似的 App 状态数据。

3. 照片处理

照片分析在设备本地完成。PhoPick 不会把你的照片上传到云端进行分析。当你删除照片时，删除操作会通过 Apple Photos 框架执行，并需要你的确认。根据 Apple 系统行为，删除后的照片可能仍会出现在「最近删除」中。

4. 购买与订阅

PhoPick 可能通过 Apple App Store 提供订阅或 App 内购买。付款、续订、退款和支付凭证由 Apple 处理。PhoPick 使用 StoreKit 检查你是否拥有有效权益。我们不会接收或保存你的银行卡信息。

5. 分析、广告与追踪

PhoPick 不使用第三方广告 SDK，不展示广告，也不会跨其他公司拥有的 App 或网站追踪你。

6. 诊断与支持

App 可能会在设备本地生成诊断日志，用于帮助了解 App 行为。除非你在寻求支持时主动提供信息，否则这些日志不会发送给我们。如果你通过邮件联系我们，我们只会使用你提供的信息来回复你的请求。

7. 你的选择

  • 你可以在 iOS 设置中授权、限制或撤销照片图库访问权限。
  • 你可以在 Apple ID 的订阅设置中管理或取消订阅。
  • 你可以卸载 App 来移除设备上的本地 App 数据，但仍会受 iOS 备份和系统行为影响。

8. 联系我们

如果你对本隐私政策有疑问，可以通过 lzwmax@qq.com 联系我们。
